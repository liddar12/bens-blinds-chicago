Solar shades are engineered to block solar heat and UV radiation without blocking your view. Unlike standard roller shades, solar fabric is an open weave — letting you control how much light and heat enters while keeping your Chicago skyline visible.

## Why Chicago Homeowners Choose Solar Shades

South and west-facing Chicago condos receive intense direct sun. East-facing lakefront units deal with glaring morning light off Lake Michigan. Solar shades solve both problems without sacrificing the view you paid for.

- Home offices with screen glare issues
- Any room where view preservation matters
