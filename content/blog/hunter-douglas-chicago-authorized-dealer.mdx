## PowerView Motorization: Hunter Douglas's Smart Home System

Hunter Douglas PowerView is one of the most sophisticated motorized window treatment systems available. Unlike generic motorized shades that require a hub and complex programming, PowerView uses a dedicated hub that integrates natively with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, SmartThings, Control4, and Savant. Scenes can be programmed to set every shade in your home to a specific position simultaneously — 'Morning' might raise bedroom shades 50%, lower living room solar shades fully, and leave kitchen shades open. PowerView Gen 3 (the current system) uses rechargeable lithium batteries with a single USB-C charging cord that reaches all batteries without removing the shade. For Chicago homeowners building a smart home, PowerView is the most seamlessly integrated window treatment system available.
